## Quick Reference: Top 10 Rule Categories

1. **Accessibility (CRITICAL)** — 4.5:1 contrast, keyboard nav, alt text, aria-labels
2. **Touch & Interaction (CRITICAL)** — 44×44px minimum, 8px gaps, clear feedback
3. **Performance (HIGH)** — WebP/AVIF images, lazy loading, <0.1 CLS
4. **Style Selection (HIGH)** — Match product type, SVG icons (no emoji), consistency
5. **Layout & Responsive (HIGH)** — Mobile-first, no horizontal scroll, viewport meta
6. **Typography & Color (MEDIUM)** — 16px base, 1.5 line-height, semantic tokens
7. **Animation (MEDIUM)** — 150–300ms duration, transform/opacity only, respect reduced-motion
8. **Forms & Feedback (MEDIUM)** — Visible labels, inline validation, clear errors
9. **Navigation (HIGH)** — Predictable back, ≤5 bottom nav items, deep linking
10. **Charts & Data (LOW)** — Accessible colors, legends, tooltips, screen reader support
